Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the shipping cost?

Within Sweden it cost around 8 Euro, or 81 SEK.

Within EU there is a flat rate of 15 Euro.

Outside of EU there is a flat rate of 25 Euro.


How long does the sipping take?

Shipping in Sweden takes 2-4 buissness days. Sending within EU takes between 2-7 buissness days and Outside of EU takes up to 30 buissness days.


Are the payments secure? Who handles them?

All payments are handled by Stripe which online services guarantees secure and safe payments with your credit card. We store no credit card data of any kind in our database.
